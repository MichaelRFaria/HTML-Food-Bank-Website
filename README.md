## Community Table Food Bank Website

A simple client-side only website created for a fictional food bank aiming to present their business to the public. The project focused on producing a well structured website with clear semantics, that is appropriately accessible and responsible.

## Outputs
The website consist of 4 separate pages:
- **index.html** - the main page, guiding the user and displaying general information on the business.
- **html/contact_us.html** - a page for sending an e-mail with any inquiries to the food bank.
- **html/gallery.html** - a page displaying a gallery of volunteers and marketing images. 
- **html/testimonials.html** - a page displaying testimonials from the community.
